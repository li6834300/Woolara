Our Story

Nature in Every Loop

Who We Are

Woolara is a modern cashmere yarn brand founded by a group of textile lovers and designers across Europe and China.

We are committed to bringing the world's finest cashmere — responsibly sourced, hand-processed, and ethically made — directly to the hands of passionate makers.

Our name stands for softness, honesty, and the warmth of connection — between people, nature, and craftsmanship.

What We Make

Why We Do It

We believe luxury should be meaningful, not wasteful.

Every strand tells a story — of land, animal, and maker. Our commitment goes beyond creating beautiful yarn; it's about preserving traditions, supporting communities, and respecting nature.

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with independent herders in Inner Mongolia who comb goats gently by hand each spring, ensuring animal welfare at every step.

Slow Fashion

We promote slow fashion principles and empower handcraft communities around the world through fair trade practices.

Quality Assurance

We insist on complete traceability, rigorous quality control, and sustainable practices at every step of our supply chain.

"To us, every strand tells a story — of land, animal, and maker."

Our Commitment

Beyond creating exceptional yarn, we're committed to transparency and sustainability. Every purchase supports traditional herding communities and contributes to preserving ancient craftsmanship techniques.

We believe that true luxury comes from knowing the story behind what you create, understanding its impact, and feeling connected to its origins.

Where Our Cashmere Comes From

Our fibers are collected from goats raised in the pristine highlands of Inner Mongolia.

The Alashan Plateau and Ordos region provide a unique climate — with harsh winters and soft, dry summers — that results in longer, finer undercoats producing some of the world's softest cashmere.

We exclusively use hand-combed fibers, harvested during the natural molting season to ensure no stress to the animals and maintain the highest fiber quality.
